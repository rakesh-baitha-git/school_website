Academics at MIM Academy

At MIM Academy, we offer a well-rounded education designed to develop both intellectual and practical skills. Our curriculum is carefully designed to encourage critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving abilities.

We offer a wide range of subjects in the fields of Science, Mathematics, Social Studies, Languages, and Arts, with special emphasis on STEM education and extracurricular enrichment programs.

We cater to students from primary to higher secondary levels, ensuring that every student receives the best possible guidance to help them succeed academically and socially.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education that nurtures both academic and co-curricular development. We follow the guidelines set by the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE) and the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHSE).

From primary education through secondary and higher secondary levels, we focus on delivering a well-rounded learning experience that includes strong foundations in core subjects and enriching activities like sports, arts, and vocational training.

Grade Level Information

Primary School (Grades 1-5): Students in these grades focus on building fundamental knowledge in subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Languages.

Secondary School (Grades 6-10): In these grades, we prepare students for the board exams while emphasizing in-depth subject knowledge and practical applications.

Higher Secondary (Grades 11-12): Students at this level prepare for their higher education and career paths, with specializations in subjects like Science, Commerce, and Humanities.
