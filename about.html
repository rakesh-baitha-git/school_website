About Us

Welcome to MIM Academy! Established in 1990, we are dedicated to providing quality education to our students. Our institution is affiliated with WBBSE & WBCHSE, offering a comprehensive learning environment that nurtures academic excellence.

Our mission is to foster holistic development, blending academic rigor with extracurricular activities, preparing our students for the challenges ahead.

At MIM Academy, we believe in the power of education to transform lives and help build a brighter future.
